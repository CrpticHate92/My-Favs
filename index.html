My Favourite Things!

I will be showing you three of my favourite things, as well as give a reason as to why they are my favourites.

My Favourite Game:The Legend Of Zelda Breath Of The Wild.

Breath Of The Wild, has been my favourite game for quite some time, and will probably continue to be for some time. It is an open world game, that lets you do what you want to do. You can get the strongest armor and tools, or fight the final boss with nothing but a stick and your underwear. You can also challange yourself with the many shrines and puzzles, and if you're truly insane, you can try and collect all 999 korok seeds. If you find the game to easy, you can always try the master mode. It makes the enemies even stronger, but also adds more loot to grab. The modding community is also huge, they make the usually impossible possible. You can do so much with this game, and more.

My Favourite Movie:The Lord Of The Rings Trilogy.

Lord Of The Rings, is one my favourite book series, along with other fantasy fiction. When I found out they were made into movies, I was so happy. The design of everything was amazing, the cast was perfect, and it followed the story really well. It was a long binge to watch them all, but was very worth it. It makes you imagine all these fantasy like things, that you wish were real. The highest score the three movies got on Rotton Tomatoes, was 95% and an audience score of 95%. It also got a IMDb rating of 8.8/10. My favourite genre is fantasy, and this trilogy perfectly fits it.

My Favourite Sport:Baseball.

I've been playing baseball for 12 years, and I have enjoyed it for so long. It is an easy sport to pick up from a young age, and as you get older the it challanges you more. It's not a brains over brawns and vice versa, it challanges every skill you have. You need to have good stamina, good upperbody for throwing and hitting, and lower body for all the running. You also need the right mentality, thinking 5 steps ahead to make a proper play. There are 9 different possitions you can play. 6 infield, and 3 outfield. If you have a cannon of an arm, you are best suited for the outfield. If you're quick on your feet, but have a weaker arm, Infield is better. In my personal oppinion, It's the best sport but not everyone will like it.
